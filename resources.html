My Favorite Free Web Resources

In this section I just wanted to share some of the free resources I frequently use that are available online.

Khan Academy

Khan Academy is an organization that is dedicated to free education and on their website their are videos, articles, and interactive quizzes. It's an amazing resource for learning a plethora of different subjects.

The Organic Chemistry Tutor

The Organic Chemistry tutor is a tutor in a wide array of subjects. Thier videos are straight forwards and detailed, delivering a wealth of information in a short time. Many worked examples included in videos.

Physics Ninja

Physics is a tutor focused on physics and is a great resource for learning and reviewing.

Professor Dave Explains

Professor Dave Explains is a tutor for a large variety of topics, providing many starightforward videos explaining science.

Open Stax

Open Stax is an organization that provides free e-textbooks, you can nearly find text books covering any topic on this site.
